Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken cutlet coated with crispy panko. Serve with hot rice and tonkatsu sauce for a restaurant-worthy dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Panko/Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Hammer the chicken/pork.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Put the chicken in flour, then in egg, and then in the breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Slice and serve.

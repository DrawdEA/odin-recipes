Carbonara Recipe

Description

Pasta carbonara is one of those simple dinners we should all know how to make. It’s the perfect go-to for a busy weeknight, and it’s also a dish fit for the weekend or even to serve to guests.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Pasta of Choice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushroom
  • Hotdog/Ham/Bacon
  • Nestle All-Purpose Cream
  • Elaska Evaporada
  • Grated Cheese

Procedure

  1. Add water to a casserole. Add a tablespoon of salt and a bit of oil. Then once it boils, add the pasta.
  2. Wait for 10 minutes until al dente. Drain the pasta. Add olive oil to the pasta.
  3. Add oil to a pan. Then, add garlic and onions.
  4. Add in the bacon or whatever meat you may have. Once cooked, add in the mushroom.
  5. Pour the all-purpose cream and Evaporada in the pan. Shortly after, add in the grated cheese. Then, gradually add in an egg yolk while mixing. Add pepper.
  6. Mix in the pasta and serve.
